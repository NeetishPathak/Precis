Article : https://hbr.org/2007/07/the-making-of-an-expert

Takeaways:
Who is an expert ?
    He always produces superior results than his peers
    He is able to produce concrete results
    He results can be replicated and measured in lab

How to become an expert ?
    Practice deliberately
    Take time to Practice
    FInd a mentor and coach and practice with them
